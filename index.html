About Us

AetherTech Solutions is a technology consultancy and software solutions firm headquartered in Kuala Lumpur. We serve payment intermediaries, merchant operators, and digital service businesses on enterprise-grade implementations.

Our team carries deep, practical experience across payment infrastructure, financial systems integration, and managed platform operations, spanning the full engagement lifecycle.

We maintain established working relationships with banks and payment gateway providers, enabling direct integration pathways and streamlined compliance processes for the clients we support.

Our work sits at the convergence of software engineering and financial technology, delivered to the standards that regulated and high-transaction environments demand.
